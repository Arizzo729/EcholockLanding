Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

EchoUnite ("we", "our", or "us") operates the EchoUnite mobile application. This page informs users about how information is collected, used, and protected when using the EchoUnite platform.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information such as name, username, or email
  • Messages sent between users through the platform
  • Job listings, offers, and task information
  • Basic usage data required to operate the application

How Information Is Used

The information collected may be used to:

  • Provide and maintain the EchoUnite service
  • Enable communication between users
  • Allow users to post jobs or find work
  • Improve app functionality and reliability
  • Respond to support requests

Data Sharing

EchoUnite does not sell personal information to third parties. Information may be shared only when necessary to operate the platform, comply with legal obligations, or protect the security of users.

User Content

Information posted publicly within the platform such as job listings, user profiles, or messages shared with other users may be visible to those users participating in the platform.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

EchoUnite is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support page.
